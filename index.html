Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our durians

When is the peak durian season?

Peak season runs from July to September, with daily fresh harvests from our Balik Pulau orchard. However, we maintain year-round availability through careful cultivation practices.

Do you deliver internationally?

Unfortunately, we do not offer international shipping at the moment. We are currently only available in Penang Island and Mainland Malaysia unless it's a bulk order

Which variety should I try first?

For first-time durian lovers, we recommend starting with Musang King or D24. Both offer the perfect balance of flavor and creaminess that showcases why durian is the king of fruits. If you are a fan of sweet and creamy durians, we recommend trying Khun Poh. If you are a fan of bitter and tangy durians, we recommend trying D24.

How do you ensure freshness during delivery?

We used good grades /airtight containers to ensure the durians are fresh and safe during delivery.
